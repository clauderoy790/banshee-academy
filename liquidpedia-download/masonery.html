    Improved Masonry
    Increases the armor and hit points of Human buildings.
    Researched at : Lumber Mill
    		  Hotkey : M 
    Cost : 125 50  Research Time:  60
    Hit Point Bonus : 10%
    Armor Bonus : 1
    Advanced Masonry

    Requires : Keep
    Hit Point Bonus : 20%
    Armor Bonus : 2

    		Cost : 175 100  Research Time:  90
    Requires : Castle
    Hit Point Bonus : 30%

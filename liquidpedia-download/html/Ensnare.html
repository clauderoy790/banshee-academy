    Ensnare

    From Liquipedia Warcraft Wiki

    Ensnare is a common active ability and Disable that traps and immobilizes an enemy unit or hero for a period of time, making it easier to attack and kill. It also brings air units down to the ground, enabling ground attacks to damage them.

    This article is for the caster form version of this unit, for the crow version see: Druid of the Talon (Crow).

    The Druid of the Talon is rarely seen but can be useful in several situations. Night Elves can have difficulty vs. heavy armored units. Druids of the Talon can cast Faerie Fire that reduces the enemy's armor and allows the Night Elves to have a much easier time attacking them. Another especially powerful use for Druids of the Talon is Cyclone. Cyclone renders a unit useless for 20 seconds. Druids of the Talon can also be useful in Storm Crow form for reaching islands, for scouting the map, and even for attacking massed enemy air units. Storm Crows can even cast Faerie Fire once you research their upgrade.

    Strategy[edit]

    The Druid of the Talon is mostly used in the match-up against Orc.

    In the Night Elf mirror, Archers and Druids of the Talon are sometimes played.

    Against Human, a timing push with Druids of the Talon can be played to counter the Human's heavy armored Footmen. This strategy only works, if the Human player has a very late tech, and can easily be countered once the Human player gets access to tier 2 units.
    If a Human players goes for tank lame, Druids of the Talon can be added to reduce the armor of the tanks.

    Spells & Abilities[edit]

    Faerie Fire
    Cast Type : Autocast  Ability Type:  Magical
    Target Type : Unit Target
    Reduces a target enemy unit's armor by 4 and gives vision of that unit.
    Cast Requirements : 45 1 s  Range:  700
    Targets Allowed : Air, Ground, Enemy, Neutral
    Duration : 70 (40) s
    Armor Reduction : 4
    Hotkey : R

    Notes

    Faerie Fire is a very good spell to use against units like Grunts or Tauren which are difficult to kill because of their armor. It is also good to use against Heroes. If the Druids of the Talon are not automatically casting Faerie Fire on the right enemy unit, cast it yourself manually. For example if you want Faerie Fire on an enemy Hero but the Druids of the Talon are not automatically targeting that hero, cast it yourself.

    Research Mark of the Talon so that you can use Faerie Fire while in Storm Crow Form.


    Crow Form
    Cast Type : Active  Ability Type:  Magical
    Target Type : No Target
    Transforms the Druid into a storm crow, giving him the ability to fly. While in Storm Crow Form he cannot cast any spells until you have researched Mark of the Talon.
    Cast Requirements : 25
    Required upgrade : Druid of the Talon Adept Training
    Hotkey : F

    Notes

    See more on the Druid of the Talon Crow Form here: Druid of the Talon (Crow)

    Cyclone
    Cast Type : Active  Ability Type:  Magical
    Target Type : Unit Target
    Tosses a target enemy unit into the air, rendering it unable to move, attack or cast spells, and stopping others from attacking or casting on it.
    Cast Requirements : 150 5 s  Range:  600
    Targets Allowed : Ground, Enemy, Neutral, Organic
    Duration : 20 (6) s
    Required upgrade : Druid of the Talon Master Training
    Hotkey : C

    Notes

    Often the enemy may run leaving behind the flying units in the Cyclone. When these units land the enemy is often not watching them at this point leaving them to you for free kills. Cyclone is a very effective counter for large easy to click on units such as Abominations, Tauren, Bears and Knights.

    Use Subgroups to cast Cyclone effectively. Good players can place an entire enemy army up in the air.

    Cyclone can be dispelled.

    Cyclone cannot be stolen with Spell Steal

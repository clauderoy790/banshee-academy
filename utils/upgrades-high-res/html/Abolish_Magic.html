    Abolish Magic

    From Liquipedia Warcraft Wiki

    Abolish Magic is an autocast dispel ability and upgrade that removes positive buffs from enemy units, and negative buffs from friendly units. It also deals damage to summoned units.

    Version History[edit]

    Balance Patch History
    VersionReleaseBalance Changes
    1.30.02018-08-08
    • Abolish Magic damage against summoned units reduced from 300 to 250.
    1.172004-09-20
    • Abolish Magic no longer auto-dispels hero spells.
    • Abolish Magic no longer removes positive buffs on friendlies or negative buffs on enemies when manually cast (it already did this for autocast).
    • Default autocast spells (like Abolish Magic or Bloodlust) that require research will now have autocast enabled when the research completes.
    1.122003-07-31
    • The creep version of Abolish Magic has had its mana cost increased from 50 to 75.
